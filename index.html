Management of a bicycle rental network

This project was made for the discipline of "Object-Oriented Programming" and intends to simulate a system that manages a network of bicycle rentals, along with the full range of functions necessary for its correct functioning. It allows any type of user to go to one of the several stations distributed throughout the city and there find terminals where the various operations are performed. From registration (which may be changed later) and purchase of a BikeShare card, until the effective rental of a bicycle. There will be users who intend to register and take advantage of the various types of annuities available and the less assiduous people who, without the purpose of registering, only intend to make occasional rentals. In this case, they need to purchase rental tickets. All possible scenarios will depend on the momentary availability of bikes on the docks of the station. The program will also have a slope where maintenance of the entire system is processed. All the managers have a specific card that when read by the terminal gives access to other functionalities. Organize and distribute the various existing bicycles (they can be left by users in stations other than the station where they have been rented leading to an imbalance in their distribution), recycle the purchased ticket codes and debit non-complying users maximum delivery of 24 hours. The technologie used in this project was Java(JavaFX).
